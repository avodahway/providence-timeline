Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your Providence Timeline account and the entries stored under that account.

Before You Request Deletion

If you want a copy of your entries, sign in to the app first and use the JSON or Markdown export buttons. Deleted accounts and entries may not be recoverable.

How To Request Deletion

  1. Send a deletion request to the app owner through the support channel used to distribute Providence Timeline.
  2. Include the email address used for your Providence Timeline account.
  3. The app owner will verify the request and remove the Supabase user account.

What Gets Deleted

Deleting the Supabase user account removes the account record and associated Providence Timeline entries through database cascade rules.

What May Remain

Temporary logs, backups, or security records may remain for a limited time if retained by infrastructure providers. These are not used to rebuild your account.
