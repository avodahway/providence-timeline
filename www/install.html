Install Providence Timeline

Add Providence Timeline to your home screen or desktop so it opens like a quiet private app, not another browser tab.

iPhone or iPad

  1. Open Providence Timeline in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button.
  3. Choose Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add.

Android

  1. Open Providence Timeline in Chrome.
  2. Tap the browser menu.
  3. Choose Install app or Add to Home screen.
  4. Confirm the install.

Desktop

  1. Open Providence Timeline in Chrome or Edge.
  2. Look for the install icon in the address bar.
  3. Or open the app menu and choose Install App.
  4. Launch it from your dock, taskbar, or applications folder.

The installed app still uses your same Providence Timeline account. Your entries save online when connected, and the app shell can open even when the network is unavailable.
