Providence Timeline

Privacy Policy

Providence Timeline is designed as a private place to record memories, prayers, later reflections, scripture references, and connected life events.

Effective date: June 29, 2026

Information We Collect

When you create an account, Providence Timeline stores your email address for authentication. When you use the app, it stores the entries you create, including titles, dates, notes, seasons, story arcs, tags, people, locations, scripture references, reflections, and connections between entries.

How Your Information Is Used

Your information is used to provide login, cloud saving, editing, exporting, and timeline features. The app does not sell your personal information.

Where Data Is Stored

Account and entry data are stored with Supabase. Access is restricted so signed-in users can read, create, update, and delete their own entries.

Exports

The app includes JSON and Markdown export tools so you can keep a personal copy of your entries.

Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated entries. See the account deletion instructions for the current process.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact the app owner through the support channel used to distribute Providence Timeline.
